For You

I’m not a poet, and I don’t know how to write shayari.
But I know music.

And somehow, every time I hear certain songs, I see you — your smile, your silence, your strength, your love, your softness.

So I tried to put my feelings into something I understand — songs.
Each one of them carries a piece of my heart for you.

This is not a playlist.
This is my heart, translated into music.

Please enter the secret password I shared with you.
